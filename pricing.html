Questions? Answered.

Can I switch between Basic and Pro anytime?
+
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades activate immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
+
Yes! New accounts get a 14-day Pro trial automatically — no payment required to start.
How does annual billing save money?
+
Annual billing gives you 20% off. Basic goes from ₹300/mo to ₹240/mo (₹2,880/yr). Pro goes from ₹500/mo to ₹400/mo (₹4,800/yr) — saving ₹1,200 a year.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept UPI, net banking, debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and Paytm. All payments are processed securely.
Can station owners use these plans?
+
Station owners have a separate registration flow with access to the Owner Panel. The plans above are for EV user accounts. Contact us for station owner pricing.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
+
Your account and data are preserved for 90 days after cancellation. You can reactivate any time within that window without losing anything.
